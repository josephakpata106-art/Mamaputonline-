DOCUMENTATION
For this project, I designed and built a responsive food ordering website called MamaPutOnline using only HTML and CSS.
My goal was to create a simple, attractive,and easy-to-use website where users can browse different Nigerian meals,
view prices and special offers, and get in touch through a contact form.
I designed the navigation bar, hero section, product catalog, promotional section, contact page, and footer,
while maintaining a consistent color scheme, modern typography, and responsive layout.
I also added hover effects and clean styling to make the website more interactive and provide a smooth user experience across different screen sizes.
